## **The Problem**

JSON was designed for machines, not language models. It wastes tokens on structural elements, requires additional parsing logic, and doesn't match the natural reasoning patterns of LLMs.

APIs have been designed for decades with machine parsing in mind \- rigid, explicit structures that computers can easily process. But when our consumers are language models, these formats introduce unnecessary overhead.
